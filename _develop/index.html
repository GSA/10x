The 10x process What happens if your idea is selected?

First, send 10x your idea.

10x is currently accepting ideas on a rolling basis from all City of Sacramento employees. It’s easy to submit an idea to 10x and we want to hear yours. Either send 10x your idea via our submission form, or send it to us as an email. We review ideas for funding a few times a year. Submit your idea before August 15, 2019 to be considered for our next round of evaluations in early September.

10x is looking for ideas that:

  • Create cost savings for City departments.
  • Improve the performance of City operations, programs, or services.
  • Have an impact across City government or in our neighborhoods.

How should you present your idea? Your idea should:

  • Be no more than three sentences long.
  • Demonstrate a clear understanding of the problem you want to solve.
  • Focus on developing or implementing an innovative solution.
  • Be as clear and descriptive as possible.

As we scale this program it’s important that 10x hears ideas that impact all aspects of City government. Your idea is valuable. If you need help putting together an idea or have questions about what we’re looking for, email us at 10x@innovatesac.org. We’re happy to connect you with a member of our Innovation Team to review what you have and make sure your idea is clear.

Develop your best 10x idea →

Then, 10x goes to work.

10x evaluates ideas and supports those with the greatest potential for impact.

A few times a year, 10x collects all the ideas and evaluates them for development and implementation. The most promising ideas receive the support needed to turn them into reality.

Your idea will be evaluated based on the following 3 criteria, each given equal weight:

  • Clarity. Does the idea demonstrate an understanding of a real problem and communicate it clearly?
  • Cost Savings. Does the idea have the potential to save money for the City?
  • Impact. Will solving this problem make City government more effective or improve quality of life for City residents?

If selected, 10x supports incremental development of the idea, step-by-step, into a sustainable project. This iterative approach, based on modern technology and investment practices, improves the odds that the idea will be successfully implemented while minimizing costs.

10x projects are managed and staffed by project teams within the City's Innovation Team within the Office of Innovation & Economic Development.
