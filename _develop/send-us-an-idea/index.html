Send us an idea

10x wants ideas from City employees that that can mprove the performance of City operations and quality of life in our neighborhoods.

Propose a new ideausing our form

or email your idea directly to 10x@innovatesac.org.

Eligibility

10x is currently accepting ideas from all City of Sacramento employees – Submit your idea before August 15, 2019 to be considered for our next round of evaluations in early September.

What makes a good idea?

10x is looking for ideas that:

  • Create cost savings for City departments.
  • Improve the performance of City operations, programs, or services.
  • Have an impact across City government or in our neighborhoods.

Your idea should:

  • Be no more than three sentences long.
  • Demonstrate a clear understanding of the problem you want to solve.
  • Focus on creating a new product or service that is good for government.
  • Be as clear and descriptive as possible.

Need some help?

If you’d like help putting together an idea or have questions about what we’re looking for, email us at 10x@innovatesac.org. We’re happy to connect you with a technology expert or someone to review what you have and make sure your idea is clear.
